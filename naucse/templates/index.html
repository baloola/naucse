Planned courses

7th Python beginners course - Spring 2024

It is aimed for complete beginners without any previous experience in programming. We will go through basic Python syntax, data types and structures and we will work on two projects along the way. You can find the full curriculum in the course details. The course will be run for two hours each week in a virtual classroom for around 25 attendees and several mentors.

If you are interested to join as an attendee, please fill the registration google form and let us know about yourself. We usually receive more applications than there are free slots. In that case we will need to select participants based on the answers from the form.

If you would like to join as a mentor, thank you, you are AWESOME! Please send us an email to pyladies.vienna@gmail.com with a short description about you and and we will get back to you as soon as possible!

If you can not attend this course but want a reminder for when we open the registrations for future courses, please submit your email to us: in this form.

Who are PyLadies?

PyLadies is an international mentorship group with a focus on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community. Our mission is to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events and social gatherings.

For whom?

Our most important activity in PyLadies Vienna are online semestral Python beginners courses. They will lead you through complete Python programming fundamentals in an entertaining way. No boring math, all lessons are taught on easily imaginable examples and even programming of simple games.

What do you need?

In order to start, you need only motivation, enough free time and your own computer on which you can start to learn programming - with permission to install new software.
