Mentorship programme

About the programme

With finishing a Beginners Python Course your coding journey does not stop! On the contrary actually. Now you are familiar with building blocks and it is on you to decide in which directions to go. There is no limit. And mentors and our mentorship programme can help you. This programme is aiming on connecting mentees with mentors based on needs and wishes of the mentee and expertise of the mentors. It would be great if you are able to define what you are looking for, but if you just want a learning buddy it is also fine.

For whom is the mentoring programme?

  • You are looking for someone with specific knowledge in a field who can help you achieve your goals.
  • You want to continue studying and want a buddy who will hold you accountable and help with tips and tricks when needed.
  • You want to land a job in IT and want someone to help you on this journey.
  • You want to jump from junior to medior and build a skills (not only technical) needed.

We have currently paused the applications to the mentorship programme because we are restructuring it. Stay tuned! Sign up for our newsletter to be informed when we relaunch the programme in the new form.
